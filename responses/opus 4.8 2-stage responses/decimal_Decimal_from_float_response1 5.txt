1. **Type preservation:** The output of `Decimal.from_float(f)` is always an instance of `Decimal`, regardless of whether the input is a `float` or an `int`.

3. **Round-trip back to float:** For any finite float `f`, converting the result back to a float (`float(Decimal.from_float(f))`) yields the original float `f` exactly.
