1. If either operand is a NaN, the result is a Decimal NaN.

2. If neither operand is NaN, the result is always one of exactly `Decimal('-1')`, `Decimal('0')`, or `Decimal('1')`.

3. If neither operand is NaN and the first operand is numerically less than the second, the result is `Decimal('-1')`.

4. If neither operand is NaN and the two operands are numerically equal, the result is `Decimal('0')`.

5. If neither operand is NaN and the first operand is numerically greater than the second, the result is `Decimal('1')`.
