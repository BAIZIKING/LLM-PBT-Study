1. For every edge with a finite capacity, the reported flow on that edge is between zero and the edge capacity, inclusive.

2. For every node other than the source and sink, total incoming flow equals total outgoing flow.

3. The returned `flow_value` equals the net flow leaving the source, i.e. total flow out of the source minus total flow into the source.

4. The returned `flow_value` also equals the net flow entering the sink, i.e. total flow into the sink minus total flow out of the sink.

5. The returned flow is optimal: in the residual graph induced by the returned `flow_dict`, there is no augmenting path from the source to the sink; equivalently, the capacity of the corresponding minimum cut equals `flow_value`.
