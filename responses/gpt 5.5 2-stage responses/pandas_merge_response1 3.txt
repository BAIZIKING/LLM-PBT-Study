4. The output row ordering respects `how`: `left` and `inner` preserve left-key order, `right` preserves right-key order, `outer` sorts keys lexicographically, and `cross` preserves left order while pairing each left row with each right row.

5. For `how='cross'`, the output has exactly `len(left) * len(right)` rows, and every left row appears paired with every right row exactly once.
