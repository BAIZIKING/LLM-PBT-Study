1. For any non-empty numeric input, the median is invariant under reordering of the data.

2. For any non-empty numeric input, the median lies between the minimum and maximum values of the input, inclusive.

3. For an odd number of data points, the median equals the middle element of the sorted data.

4. For an even number of data points, the median equals the arithmetic mean of the two middle elements of the sorted data.

5. For an empty input, the function raises `StatisticsError`.
